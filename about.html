The Woman Behind the Words

Hi, I'm Shera —
creative, writer,
woman unfolding.

I write about life's real chapters — the messy, the magical, and everything in between. Not a highlight reel. The actual journey.

I started this blog because I've always needed somewhere to put it all. Life has a way of throwing things at you that leave you thinking "why me?" — until you realise you're not the only one asking. Writing is how I process, how I connect, and honestly? How I have fun with it too.

I couldn't find enough of those stories — so I decided to write them myself.

Pull up a chair. You're right on time.
